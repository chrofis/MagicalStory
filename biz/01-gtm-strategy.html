1 Executive Summary

What We Do

Parents upload photos, choose from 170+ themes, and our AI generates fully illustrated storybooks where their child is the hero. Every illustration features the child's actual face with consistent appearance across all pages.

Why It Matters

Wonderbly sold for EUR 99M using templates. We use AI for true personalization: real faces, local stories, Swiss German dialects. Better product, better unit economics, defensible technology moat.

Business Model

Freemium. First story free (digital). Printed books CHF 38-96 via Gelato. Additional digital stories CHF 9.90. Gift cards CHF 39/59/79. Future: subscription for families.

Target Market

Swiss parents of children aged 2-8. Start canton-by-canton in German-speaking Switzerland, expand to DACH (months 4-6), then global English markets (months 7-12).

10 Risk Mitigation

Abuse & Fraud

  • Cloudflare Turnstile on free story creation
  • One free story per verified email
  • Rate limiting per IP address
  • Stripe Radar for payment fraud

Privacy & Compliance

  • Photos encrypted, deleted after avatar generation
  • GDPR and Swiss nDSG compliant
  • Privacy-first analytics (Plausible)
  • 72-hour deletion request response

Quality Assurance

  • Entity repair workflow (multi-pass)
  • Automatic low-score image regeneration
  • Monthly Gelato print sample QA
  • Customer complaint tracking system

Cost Control

  • AI cost per story: CHF 2-4.50 (monitored)
  • Budget caps per user for free tier
  • Fallback to cheaper models when possible
  • Volume pricing negotiation at 500+ orders/month
