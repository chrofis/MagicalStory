Competitive Moat

Six reinforcing layers that make town stories defensible.

Research Effort

Each town requires local knowledge — landmarks, traditions, dialect. A competitor needs to research 50+ towns to match us.

Content Creation

50 town pages x 5 story ideas = 250 unique concepts. Plus illustrations of each town's landmarks. Months of work.

SEO Compounding

First to rank for "personalisiertes kinderbuch zurich" owns that keyword for years. SEO is a compounding advantage.

Local Trust

Parents who see their town in our product trust us more. This trust doesn't transfer to a competitor who arrives later.

Network Effects

More parents in a town = more UGC = more social proof = more parents. Each town builds its own local flywheel.

Data Advantage

We learn which towns, themes, and stories perform best. This data informs where to invest next. Competitors start blind.
