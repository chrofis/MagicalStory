# Content & UGC Strategy

Content serves three purposes:

1. **SEO**: Drive organic traffic
2. **Social proof**: Build trust through real stories
3. **Education**: Help parents understand the value

## UGC Strategy — Without Hiring Child Actors

### The Hooray Heroes Insight

Hooray Heroes built their entire business on UGC — 90%+ of their advertising is user-generated content. Their secret: emotional reactions from parents AND children when they receive/read the personalized book. One nano-influencer campaign generated 1,394 Instagram posts, 6,147 Instagram stories, and reached 15M followers across 8 markets.

### MagicalStory's UGC Approach: "The Story Behind the Story"

We do NOT need to film children. Here's what works:

### UGC Type 1: The Parent's Reaction

- Film the parent's face as they flip through their child's story for the first time
- Parents crying, laughing, being amazed
- No child's face needed — the parent's emotion IS the content
- **Why it works:** Emotional contagion, social proof, authenticity

### UGC Type 2: The Book Reveal (Child-Safe)

- Film: Hands holding the book, flipping pages
- Show: The illustrations with the child as hero
- Film: Child's voice (not face) saying "That's ME!"
- **Why it works:** Product showcase + emotional audio
- **Rule:** Never require or encourage parents to show children's faces

### UGC Type 3: The Creation Process

- Screen recording: Parent uploading photo, choosing theme
- Time-lapse: Story being generated (satisfying "making of")
- Before/after: Photo -> illustrated character
- **Why it works:** Demonstrates the magic, reduces uncertainty

### UGC Type 4: The Gift Moment

- Film: Gift being wrapped, gift card being written
- Film: Parent handing book to grandparent/friend for reaction
- Film: Adult recipients' reactions (no children needed)
- **Why it works:** Gift-giving emotion, occasion marketing

### UGC Type 5: The Illustration Showcase

- Parents share favorite illustrations from their stories
- Side-by-side: Real photo vs. story illustration
- Art style comparisons (same child in watercolor vs. Pixar 3D)
- **Why it works:** Visual content performs best on Instagram

## UGC Collection System

### Step 1: In-Product Prompt

After story completion, show:

> "Your story is ready! Love it? Share your experience and get CHF 5 off your next story."

- One-tap share to Instagram Stories
- Pre-populated hashtag: #MagicalStory #MeineGeschichte
- Template overlay for Instagram Stories (branded frame)

### Step 2: Post-Purchase Email

3 days after print delivery:

> "Has [Child's name] seen their story yet? We'd love to hear about the moment. Share a 15-second video of your reaction and get CHF 10 off your next order."

- Link to submission form (no children's faces required)
- Clear guidelines: "Share YOUR reaction — we love seeing parents surprised"

### Step 3: Hashtag Monitoring

- Monitor #MagicalStory, #MeineGeschichte, #magicalstorych
- DM best content creators asking permission to repost
- Feature on website "From Our Families" section

### Step 4: Micro-Influencer Program

- **Target:** Swiss parent bloggers with 1K-10K followers
- **Offer:** Free printed book + CHF 50 if they create 1 Instagram post + 2 Stories
- **Requirement:** Show the book (not the child's face required), share honest reaction
- **Scale:** 20-30 micro-influencers in first 3 months
- **Expected reach:** 50K-150K Swiss parents

### Step 5: "Story of the Month" Feature

- Monthly: Feature one family's story on the website and social media
- With permission: Share the story theme, illustrations, and parent quote
- Prize: Free hardcover book for the featured family
- Creates ongoing UGC incentive

## Legal and Ethical UGC Guidelines

### Children's Privacy

- NEVER require or incentivize photos/videos of children's faces
- All UGC guidelines explicitly state: "Share YOUR reaction, not your child's face"
- Any UGC featuring children must have explicit parental consent
- Switzerland's data protection law (nDSG) applies — comply with it
- Right to deletion: Parents can request removal of any shared content at any time

### Consent Process

- Written release form (digital) before using any UGC
- Clearly state where content will be used (social media, ads, website)
- Option to remain anonymous (just show book, not parent)
- 30-day withdrawal period

### What we will NEVER do

- Hire child actors
- Use stock photos of children as "testimonials"
- Create fake UGC
- Pressure parents to share children's images
- Use children's faces in ads without explicit, documented consent

## Social Media Content Strategy

### Instagram (Primary Social Channel)

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Product showcases (illustrations, book photos, story previews)
- 25% UGC reposts (parent reactions, book reveals)
- 20% Behind-the-scenes (AI creation process, printing, team)
- 10% Educational/Parenting tips (reading with kids, theme explanations)
- 5% Community (polls, questions, themed days)

**Posting Frequency:**
- Feed: 4-5 posts/week
- Stories: Daily (mix of all categories)
- Reels: 2-3/week (story flip-throughs, creation process)

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Primary: #MagicalStory #MeineGeschichte #PersonalisiertesKinderbuch
- Secondary: #Kinderbuch #Kindergeschichte #Geschenkidee #SchweizMitKindern
- Local: #ZuerichMitKindern #BaselFamilies #BernFamilie #[Town]MitKindern

### Facebook (Secondary Social Channel)

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Product posts (same as Instagram, optimized for Facebook)
- 30% Community engagement (polls, questions, parenting discussions)
- 20% Promotional (free story offer, gift cards, seasonal)

**Posting Frequency:**
- 3-4 posts/week
- Active in parenting groups (provide value, don't spam)

**Groups Strategy:**
- Identify top 20 Swiss parenting Facebook groups
- Join and become active member (provide parenting value)
- Share MagicalStory content only when relevant and allowed
- Create own group: "Schweizer Eltern — Vorlesen & Geschichten" (build community)

## Content Production Workflow

Weekly content creation (2-3 hours/week):

1. **Monday:** Plan week's content, create captions
2. **Tuesday:** Generate story previews/illustrations for social
3. **Wednesday:** Film/edit one Reel (creation process or book showcase)
4. **Thursday:** Design social graphics (Canva or similar)
5. **Friday:** Schedule all content for the week
6. **Ongoing:** Monitor UGC, respond to comments, engage in groups

**Tools:**
- Canva: Social graphics, Story templates
- Later or Buffer: Content scheduling
- Instagram native: Reels editing
- Google Sheets: Content calendar
