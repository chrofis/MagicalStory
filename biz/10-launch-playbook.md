## Launch Week (Week 1)

### Day 1 — Monday: Soft Launch

**Morning:**
- Activate Campaign 1: Awareness "Meet Your Story" — CHF 30/day
- Activate Campaign 2: Conversion Emotional — CHF 50/day
- Post launch announcement on Instagram (carousel: "So funktioniert's")
- Post launch on Facebook page

**Evening:**
- Check first 8 hours of data: impressions, clicks, CTR
- Respond to all comments on ads and posts
- Share Instagram Story: "Tag 1 — wir sind live"

### Day 2 — Tuesday: Town Activation

**Morning:**
- Launch Campaign 3: Town-specific (Zürich, Basel, Bern) — CHF 30/day
- Check overnight performance from Day 1
- Identify any technical issues (broken flows, errors)

**Evening:**
- First CPC and CTR assessment
- Respond to all comments and DMs
- Instagram Story: First story creation behind-the-scenes

### Day 3 — Wednesday: Retargeting

**Morning:**
- Launch Campaign 5: Retargeting — CHF 20/day (if pixel data sufficient)
- Review conversion data: Any story completions?
- Kill ads with CTR <0.5% after 500 impressions

**Evening:**
- Reply to all engagement
- Adjust audience targeting based on early data

### Day 4-5 — Thursday/Friday: Optimize

- Analyze first 4 days: Best creative, best audience, best town
- Double budget on winners, pause losers
- Create 2 new ad variants based on winning patterns
- Post best illustrations on Instagram
- Start DM outreach to 5 Swiss parent micro-influencers

### Day 6-7 — Weekend: Let It Run

- Weekend ads are often cheaper — let campaigns run
- Monitor for issues but don't over-optimize
- Prepare Week 2 content calendar
- Write first blog post draft

## Emergency Playbook

### Scenario: CAC Too High (>CHF 20 per story completion)

1. **Pause** all campaigns except the single best performer
2. **Audit** ad creative: Is the value proposition clear? Is the CTA strong?
3. **Audit** landing page: Can you create a story in <3 clicks? Is "free" obvious?
4. **Test** radically different creative (video vs image, emotional vs rational)
5. **Check** technical: Is the pixel firing? Are conversions tracking correctly?
6. **Ask** early users: What almost stopped you from creating a story?

### Scenario: Stories Created But Nobody Buys Print

1. **Survey** first 50 users: "Why didn't you order a print?"
2. **Check** pricing perception: Show competitor prices for anchoring
3. **Add** limited-time first-print discount (15% off, 7-day window)
4. **Improve** print preview: 3D book mockup, show physical quality
5. **Add** urgency: "Bestelle innerhalb von 7 Tagen und spare 15%"
6. **Test** print upsell in the story completion flow (not just email)

### Scenario: Free Tier Being Abused

1. **Monitor** patterns: Same IP, similar emails, high volume from one source
2. **Tighten** Turnstile difficulty
3. **Require** email verification before story generation (not just signup)
4. **Block** known disposable email providers (maintain blocklist)
5. **Add** browser fingerprinting as secondary check
6. **Last resort:** Phone verification for free stories

### Scenario: Negative PR / Privacy Concerns

1. **Respond** within 2 hours with factual, calm statement
2. **Publish** detailed blog post: "How MagicalStory Protects Your Child's Data"
3. **Highlight** specific protections: encryption, deletion, nDSG/GDPR compliance
4. **Offer** immediate data deletion to any concerned parent
5. **Consider** third-party security audit for credibility
6. **Update** FAQ with additional privacy questions

### Scenario: Competitor Copies Town Stories Feature

1. **Don't panic** — we have first-mover advantage in 40+ towns
2. **Accelerate** town page creation (scale to 100+)
3. **Double down** on local community building
4. **Emphasize** Swiss identity: "Born in Switzerland, designed for your town"
5. **Create** deeper town content: partnerships with local schools, libraries

## Go/No-Go Decision Points

### After Week 4: Continue or Pivot?

**Continue scaling if:**
- 200+ stories created
- 15+ print orders
- At least 1 ad set with CPA <CHF 10
- Positive user feedback (no major product issues)
- At least 3 organic story completions (SEO starting to work)

**Pivot approach if:**
- <50 stories created despite CHF 2,000+ spend
- 0 print orders
- All ad sets CPA >CHF 25
- Significant product issues reported by users
- Very high bounce rate on landing pages (>80%)

**Pivot options:**
- Change positioning (e.g., gift-first instead of parent-first)
- Change creative strategy entirely (video-first)
- Target different audience segment
- Adjust pricing (lower entry point)
- Focus on one town deeply instead of 20 broadly

### After Month 3: Expand or Consolidate?

**Expand to DACH if:**
- 1,500+ stories created in Switzerland
- 100+ print orders
- ROAS >2x on best campaigns
- Clear winning creative formula
- Positive NPS / user reviews
- Swiss content SEO ranking for target keywords

**Consolidate Switzerland if:**
- Metrics are positive but not stellar
- Still optimizing creative/audience
- Product improvements needed first
- Cash constraints
