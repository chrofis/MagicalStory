Ad Creative Strategy

5 creative types designed for scroll-stopping engagement

Type 1

Before/After Transformation

1080x1080 IG Feed FB Feed

Split-screen: real child photo transforms into illustrated story character. The IKEA effect in visual form — parents see what they could own. Brain processes faces first, then reconciles photo vs. illustration.

15s
Type 2

Story Flip-Through Video

1080x1920 IG Reels Stories

15-second overhead shot flipping through printed book. Peak-end rule: best pages create outsized impression. Feels like organic review/unboxing. Video drives cheaper CPMs.

Zuerich
Type 3

Town Hook

1080x1080 FB Feed IG Feed

Illustrated town landmark with child character. Local identity triggers strong emotional response. Pattern interrupt: "Why is my town in a children's book?" Shares well in local Facebook groups.

Type 4

Parent Quote / UGC Style

1080x1920 Stories Reels

Real parent quotes or child reaction videos. UGC aesthetic bypasses ad blindness. Low-fi is better — polished UGC looks fake. Lowest production cost of all types.

Type 5

Gift Occasion

Carousel FB Feed IG Feed

Gift-wrapped book with seasonal context. Activates "gift budget" mental accounting. Solves gift anxiety: "What do I get a kid who has everything?" Shows both book + gift card option.

Policy Note

Meta restricts children's images in ads. All creatives must use illustrated characters, not real photographs. The "Before/After" creative should use artistic style for both sides, or show the parent's phone screen displaying the photo (indirect depiction).
