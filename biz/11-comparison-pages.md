# Comparison Pages Strategy

Detailed outlines for 8 comparison/listicle pages to capture high-intent search traffic.

---

## General Principles

- **Be honest.** Acknowledge competitor strengths (Pratfall effect builds trust). Readers who sense bias will leave.
- **Lead with the user's need**, not our product. Start each page by framing what the reader is looking for.
- **Specific over generic.** Include exact feature counts, pricing, screenshots if possible.
- **Each page targets a keyword.** Title tag and H1 are keyword-optimized.
- **Include FAQ schema.** 3-5 questions per page for Google rich results.
- **CTA is always "try free."** Low commitment, high conversion.
- **Length:** 1,500-2,500 words each (enough depth to rank, not so much it bores).
- **Internal links:** Link to pricing, relevant theme pages, and town pages.

**Who Should Choose MagicalStory:**
