**STEP 3: CHECK THE STORY BEAT**

Only check these things (using SCENE_HINT + IMAGE_PROMPT as the source of truth):

- Correct broad clothing category? (medieval ≠ modern, winter coat ≠ t-shirt, costume ≠ casual)

- Correct setting/location?

- Correct weather/lighting? (night = dark, snow = snow)

- Emotion matches the scene? (smiling during danger = wrong, scared during calm = wrong)

Only flag when two characters listed in DECLARED INTERACTIONS as interacting face away from each other, or a character listed as watching a specific named object is looking elsewhere. Compound mirrors count too — if the declared interaction says "right foot forward, left arm swept back" and the image shows "left foot forward, right arm swept back", that is the same pose reflected. Do NOT raise a `wrong_interaction` issue for any mirror case.

- Exact colors of ANY element — clothing, objects, vehicles, hair. Only the broad category matters (modern vs medieval, red vs blue). Shade variants (lilac vs purple, crimson vs red, light-blue vs blue) never deduct.
- Clothing details: patterns, fabric type, accessories (backpack present/absent), specific garment (hoodie vs sweatshirt).
