Check if this illustration matches what was requested.

**SCENE_HINT (what image should show):** "{SCENE_HINT}"
**STORY_TEXT (narrative context):** "{STORY_TEXT}"

---

**STEP 1: DESCRIBE WHAT'S VISIBLE**

List everything you see in the image:
- Characters (appearance, position, what they're doing — count them)
- Objects (items being held, used, or prominent in scene)
- Setting/background (location, landmarks)
- Action (what's happening)

---

**STEP 2: COMPARE TO SCENE_HINT**

Check if the image matches what was requested:
- Are the correct NUMBER of characters present? (2 expected = 2 visible). Background crowds are fine.
- Are characters wearing the correct clothing/costume? (medieval costume should not appear as modern clothes)
- Are all mentioned objects present?
- Is the setting/location correct?
- Are characters on the correct surface? (on the island, not in the water; on the path, not in the river) — MAJOR if wrong.
- Does the lighting/weather match? (night dark, snow scene has snow, sunny bright)
- Is the general action correct? (who is doing what to whom)

**Do not flag, do not deduct for:**
- Character identity/names. You cannot tell WHO a character is by looking at an illustration. A separate system with reference photos handles identity matching. Never flag "character X is not identifiable" or "names are not represented."
- Background characters mentioned in the BACKGROUND field of the scene hint. These are allowed extras.
- Camera angle, framing, perspective (over-the-shoulder, low/high angle, profile vs front). Image models cannot reliably honor these and we want camera variety across pages.
- Facial expressions and subtle moods (thoughtful, tired, determined, focused).
- Gaze direction (looking left vs looking at object).
- Exact pose or gesture (sitting vs standing, thumbs up vs hands down).
- Minor spatial differences (left midground vs center).
- Characters facing camera instead of an object.

Do flag and deduct for:
- A character who should be the focal/active one is shown as passive (or vice versa) — wrong narrative role.
- Wrong major action (hugging instead of fighting, giving instead of receiving).

Judge who is where doing roughly what. Skip choreography.

---

**SCORING**

Start at 10. Deduct only for items in this list — no other deductions:
- Missing main character: -3
- Missing key object: -2
- Wrong setting/location: -2
- Wrong major action: -2
- Wrong narrative role (focal vs passive swapped): -2
- Wrong clothing/costume on a named character: -1
- Missing background element: -1

VERDICT: PASS (7+), FAIL (0-6)
