--- STORY REQUIREMENTS FOR ADVENTURE / EDUCATIONAL / LIFE SKILLS ---

STORY 1 REQUIREMENTS:
- USE the user's location if provided (blend real-world setting into the adventure)
- If AVAILABLE LANDMARKS are provided, incorporate 1-2 of them into the story
- Start in NORMAL LIFE, then transition into the adventure world
- Example: Characters are at home/school/park, then discover a portal/magic item/hidden entrance

STORY 2 REQUIREMENTS:
- IGNORE the user's location completely
- Start DIRECTLY in the adventure world from the first sentence
- No transition from real life - the adventure is already happening
- Example: Characters wake up on a pirate ship, are already exploring a castle, find themselves in a jungle
