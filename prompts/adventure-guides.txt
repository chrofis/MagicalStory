# Adventure Theme Guides
# Format: [theme-id] followed by content until next [theme-id]
# These guides indicate costume requirements and story guidance for adventure themes
# NOTE: Specific outfit details are NOT defined here - the AI creates distinct costumes per character
# following the "Distinct Costume Colors" rule in the story generation prompt.

[pirate]
COSTUME: costumed:pirate (mandatory for adventure scenes)

Story entry points (pick one or combine):
- Full pirate adventure from start to finish
- Kids playing pirates in the backyard/park with makeshift props
- Dress-up party or costume day at school
- Dream sequence where kids become real pirates
- Imagination play that comes to life
- Time travel or magic portal to the golden age of piracy
- A pirate-themed playground or attraction
- Reading a pirate book that pulls them into the story
- Finding a treasure map that starts the adventure
- Museum exhibit that comes to life

Story guidance:
- Set on ships, tropical islands, or coastal towns
- Include treasure maps, sea voyages, friendly sea creatures (dolphins, parrots, crabs)
- Pirates as fun adventurers, not scary villains
- Focus on teamwork, exploration, and problem-solving
- Key props: treasure chest, rolled-up map, parrot, ship wheel, compass, telescope
