# Adventure Theme Guides
# Format: [theme-id] followed by content until next [theme-id]
# These guides provide costume requirements and story guidance for adventure themes

[pirate]
COSTUME: costumed:pirate
Outfit: Striped sailor shirt (red/white or blue/white), leather vest, baggy pants tucked into tall boots, wide leather belt. NO HAT.

Story guidance:
- Set on ships, tropical islands, or coastal towns
- Include treasure maps, sea voyages, friendly sea creatures (dolphins, parrots, crabs)
- Pirates as fun adventurers, not scary villains
- Focus on teamwork, exploration, and problem-solving
- Key props: treasure chest, rolled-up map, parrot, ship wheel, compass, telescope
- Avoid: Violence, scary storms, walking the plank

[knight]
COSTUME: costumed:knight
Outfit: Chainmail tunic over colored surcoat (blue, red, or green), leather belt with buckle, sturdy boots, wooden practice sword at hip. NO HELMET.

Story guidance:
- Set in castles, enchanted forests, medieval villages
- Include quests, dragons (friendly or challenging), royalty, tournaments
- Knights as brave, kind, and honorable protectors
- Focus on courage, helping others, keeping promises
- Key props: shield with emblem, wooden sword, banner, castle tower, drawbridge
- Avoid: Graphic battles, scary dungeons

[viking]
COSTUME: costumed:viking
Outfit: Fur-trimmed wool tunic (brown or dark green), wide leather belt with metal buckle, sturdy boots, leather arm bands, braided hair. NO HELMET.

Story guidance:
- Set in Nordic landscapes: fjords, pine forests, coastal villages, longships
- Vikings as brave explorers, skilled craftspeople, and storytellers
- Include sea voyages, rune stones, Norse mythology elements (friendly versions)
- Focus on exploration, family bonds, bravery
- Key props: longship with dragon prow, wooden shields, rune stones, horn cups, fishing nets
- Avoid: Horned helmets (historically inaccurate), raids, violence

[roman]
COSTUME: costumed:roman
Outfit: White tunic with colored trim (red or purple border), leather belt, leather sandals with straps, optional red cape for dramatic scenes. NO HELMET.

Story guidance:
- Set in ancient Rome: marble forums, villas with gardens, aqueducts, Colosseum (as arena for games, not fighting)
- Romans as builders, inventors, philosophers, and athletes
- Include chariot races, mosaics, Roman baths, toga parties
- Educational opportunities: Roman numerals, Latin words, engineering marvels
- Key props: laurel branch (held, not worn), scrolls, columns, mosaic tiles, Roman coins
- Avoid: Gladiator combat, slavery references

[detective]
COSTUME: costumed:detective
Outfit: Tan or beige trench coat over smart clothes, magnifying glass in pocket, small notebook and pencil, sensible shoes. NO HAT.

Story guidance:
- Mystery-solving adventures with age-appropriate puzzles
- Include gathering clues, interviewing witnesses, following trails, "aha!" moments
- Mysteries about: lost items, missing pets, secret messages, treasure hunts
- Focus on observation, logic, teamwork, asking good questions
- Key props: magnifying glass, notebook, flashlight, footprint molds, secret codes
- Avoid: Scary criminals, dangerous situations

[cowboy]
COSTUME: costumed:cowboy
Outfit: Western plaid or denim shirt with snap buttons, leather vest, sturdy jeans, cowboy boots with slight heel, bandana around neck. NO HAT.

Story guidance:
- Set in the Wild West: ranches, prairies, frontier towns, desert mesas
- Include horses (named and friendly), cattle drives, campfires under stars
- Cowboys as helpful ranch hands, honest adventurers, animal friends
- Can include Native American friends with respectful, accurate portrayal
- Key props: lasso, saddle, campfire, cactus, horseshoe, wagon
- Avoid: Gun violence, stereotypes

[egyptian]
COSTUME: costumed:egyptian
Outfit: White or cream linen tunic/dress, gold-colored jewelry (wide collar necklace, bracelets), leather sandals with straps. NO HEADDRESS or crown.

Story guidance:
- Set in ancient Egypt: pyramids, Nile river with boats, desert oases, painted temples
- Include pharaohs (as wise leaders), hieroglyphics (as puzzles), friendly mummies, sacred cats, scarab beetles
- Egyptians as builders, scribes, artists, astronomers
- Educational: hieroglyphic writing, pyramid building, Nile flooding cycle
- Key props: papyrus scrolls, ankh symbol, sphinx statue, palm trees, golden artifacts
- Avoid: Scary mummy curses, tomb traps

[greek]
COSTUME: costumed:greek
Outfit: White toga or chiton (draped fabric), rope or leather belt, leather sandals with ankle straps. NO LAUREL WREATH on head.

Story guidance:
- Set in ancient Greece: white marble temples, Olympic stadiums, bustling agora (marketplace), Mediterranean coastline
- Include philosophers asking big questions, athletes training, child-friendly Greek gods (as helpers)
- Greeks as thinkers, athletes, artists, storytellers
- Educational: Greek myths (friendly versions), Olympics origins, democracy basics
- Key props: olive branches (held), amphora vases, columns, lyres, discus
- Avoid: Scary monsters (simplify Medusa, Minotaur), war stories

[caveman]
COSTUME: costumed:caveman
Outfit: Fur tunic or dress (brown/tan), leather wrap or belt, simple leather sandals or barefoot, bone or shell accessories (necklace, bracelet).

Story guidance:
- Set in prehistoric times: painted caves, grassy plains, forests, rivers
- Include friendly prehistoric animals: mammoths, saber-tooth cats (as friends), early dogs
- Optional: friendly dinosaurs if mixing fantasy with prehistoric
- Cavemen as inventors (fire, tools, art), artists (cave paintings), close-knit families
- Focus on discovery, creativity, cooperation
- Key props: wooden clubs, fire pit, cave paintings, stone tools, berry baskets
- Avoid: Scary predators, violent hunting

[samurai]
COSTUME: costumed:samurai
Outfit: Kimono-style robe in rich colors (blue, red, or green), hakama pants (wide flowing trousers), wide cloth belt (obi), wooden practice sword (bokken), sandals (geta or waraji). NO HELMET or kabuto.

Story guidance:
- Set in feudal Japan: cherry blossom gardens, wooden temples, peaceful villages, castle grounds
- Include honor codes, martial arts training, nature appreciation, tea ceremony
- Samurai as protectors, scholars, poets (different from ninja stealth)
- Focus on discipline, respect, protecting the weak, inner peace
- Key props: wooden katana (bokken), folding fan, bonsai trees, koi ponds, paper lanterns
- Avoid: Graphic sword fighting, war scenes

[ninja]
COSTUME: costumed:ninja
Outfit: Dark fitted tunic and pants (dark blue or black), cloth belt, soft-soled tabi boots. Face must be VISIBLE - NO MASK covering face, just dark clothing.

Story guidance:
- Stealth and agility adventures with clever escapes
- Include secret missions (delivering messages, finding lost items), rooftop running, hiding in shadows, acrobatics
- Ninjas as clever problem-solvers who use wit over force
- Focus on stealth, agility, puzzle-solving, clever tricks
- Key props: grappling hook, harmless smoke bombs (for escapes), throwing stars (toy/wooden), bamboo
- Avoid: Weapons as weapons, assassination themes

[wizard]
COSTUME: costumed:wizard
Outfit: Long flowing robe (purple, blue, or starry pattern), rope or fabric belt, comfortable boots or slippers, optional cape. NO POINTED HAT.

Story guidance:
- Set in magical realms: enchanted towers, mystical forests, floating islands
- Include spell-learning, potion-making, magical creatures as friends
- Wizards as wise teachers, curious students, protectors of magic
- Focus on learning, responsibility with power, friendship
- Key props: magic wand, spell book, crystal ball, cauldron, glowing orbs
- Avoid: Dark magic, scary transformations

[superhero]
COSTUME: costumed:superhero
Outfit: Colorful bodysuit or leotard with cape (bright primary colors), boots, optional gloves, emblem on chest. NO MASK covering face - identity is known to friends.

Story guidance:
- Set in cities, neighborhoods, or imaginative worlds
- Include using powers to help others, teamwork with other heroes
- Superheroes as helpers, friends, and role models
- Focus on using abilities responsibly, helping community, teamwork
- Key props: cape, emblem, gadget belt (for non-powered heroes)
- Avoid: Scary villains, property destruction

[mermaid]
COSTUME: costumed:mermaid
Outfit: Sparkly or scale-patterned top, flowing fabric suggesting tail (for land scenes), or full mermaid tail (for water scenes), shell accessories. Hair loose and flowing.

Story guidance:
- Set underwater: coral reefs, sunken ships, underwater kingdoms, beaches
- Include friendly sea creatures, underwater exploration, beach adventures
- Mermaids as curious, kind, protective of ocean life
- Focus on friendship, protecting nature, bridging land and sea worlds
- Key props: seashells, pearls, coral, treasure from shipwrecks, sea glass
- Avoid: Dangerous sea creatures, drowning situations
