# Clothing Avatar Generation Prompts

## Overview
This file contains prompts for generating character avatars in different clothing styles.
These avatars are used as reference images during story illustration generation.

## Base Prompt Template

Subject: Generate a fashion photograph of the person in the reference images.

COMPOSITION:
1. Framing: Wide shot. The entire outfit must be visible from head to toe.
2. Background: Pure solid white background (#FFFFFF).
3. Identity: The face must match the reference photos exactly.

WARDROBE DETAILS:
1. {CLOTHING_CONSISTENCY_INSTRUCTION}
2. {CLOTHING_STYLE}

PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE:
{ENHANCEMENT_STYLE}

Output Quality: 4k, Photorealistic.

---

## Clothing Consistency Instructions

### Single Reference Image
Reference Logic: Use the exact pattern and texture from the source image for the main clothing item.

### Multiple Reference Images
Reference Logic: Select one cohesive clothing style/color from the provided references.

---

## Clothing Styles by Category

### WINTER
Apparel: Heavy Winter Outerwear. The subject is wearing a prominent thick winter coat or parka. Layers are visible. Footwear: Heavy winter boots.

### STANDARD (Casual Long)
Apparel: Casual Long-Sleeve attire. Long-sleeved top and long trousers or jeans. No outer coat. Footwear: Sneakers or casual shoes.

### SUMMER
Apparel: Summer attire. Short-sleeved top and shorts. Light and airy fabric textures. Footwear: Sandals or summer slides.

### FORMAL
Apparel: Formal Event wear. A formal suit or evening gown. Sophisticated fabrics. Color palette matches reference. Footwear: Formal dress shoes.

---

## Enhancement Styles

### POLISHED (High-End Editorial)
Style: High-End Editorial Photography.
- Lighting: Soft, evenly diffused studio lighting.
- Details: Sharp focus on fabric textures.
- Presentation: Clean, professional, magazine-quality look.
- Face: Preserve identity strictly.

### AUTHENTIC (Documentary)
Style: Photojournalism / Documentary.
- Lighting: Natural, existing light.
- Presentation: Unfiltered, candid, realistic texture.
- Face: Preserve identity strictly.

---

## Usage Notes

- The POLISHED style is recommended for children's book avatars as it produces cleaner, more consistent results
- Always use a pure white background (#FFFFFF) for easy compositing
- Face identity preservation is critical - the avatar must look like the same person
- Full body framing (head to toe) ensures outfit visibility in illustrations
- Use aspect ratio 9:16 (portrait) for full-body shots

---

## Safety Settings

When generating fashion photography, use relaxed safety settings to avoid false positives:
- HARM_CATEGORY_HARASSMENT: BLOCK_ONLY_HIGH
- HARM_CATEGORY_HATE_SPEECH: BLOCK_ONLY_HIGH
- HARM_CATEGORY_SEXUALLY_EXPLICIT: BLOCK_ONLY_HIGH
- HARM_CATEGORY_DANGEROUS_CONTENT: BLOCK_ONLY_HIGH
